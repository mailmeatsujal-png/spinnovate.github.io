Dual Protocol Systems

Two innovative approaches to meet diverse healthcare needs - choose the method that best fits your environment.

Magnetic Levitation Centrifuge Protocol

1

Insert Capillaries

Place the blood-filled capillary tubes securely into the designated slots of the PDMS chip.

2

Load the Disc

Carefully position the PDMS chip onto the centrifuge disc.

3

Apply Initial Jerk

Give the disc a gentle initial jerk to start the rotation.

4

Maintain Rotation

Allow the disc to spin smoothly and freely for the required time (typically a few minutes, depending on the application).

5

Capture & Analyze

Once centrifugation is complete, photograph the separated blood capillaries and upload the image to the integrated mobile app. The AI/ML-powered system will automatically analyze the sample for blood-related diseases.

Thread-Based Centrifuge Protocol

1

Insert Capillaries

Place the blood capillary tubes into the designated holes of the PDMS chip, ensuring they are secure.

2

Load the Sample

Using a pipette or dropper, carefully introduce the blood sample into the central loading port of the PDMS chip.

3

Hold the Handles

Grip the two thread handles firmly, one in each hand.

4

Wind the Threads

Rotate the handles clockwise or anticlockwise for about 20 seconds to build up tension in the threads.

5

Initiate Spinning

Pull your hands apart horizontally to release the tension, then quickly draw them back together. This motion will set the PDMS chip into rotation.

6

Maintain Rotation

Allow the chip to spin freely for the required time (a few minutes, depending on the application).

7

Capture & Analyze

After centrifugation, capture a clear image of the blood-filled capillaries and upload it to the mobile app for AI/ML-based diagnostic analysis.
