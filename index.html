Magnetic Levitation Centrifuge Protocol

1

Prepare the Sample

Securely place the blood-filled capillary tubes into the designated slots of the PDMS microfluidic chip.

2

Load the Device

Carefully position the PDMS chip onto the magnetic levitation platform. The magnetic field will automatically engage.

3

Activate the Spin

The device's automated system initiates a frictionless spin, achieving optimal separation speeds without any manual input.

Thread-Based Centrifuge Protocol

1

Prepare the Sample

Place the blood capillary tubes into the designated holes of the PDMS chip, ensuring they are secure.

2

Load Blood Sample

Using a pipette or dropper, load the blood sample into the center circular loading port of the PDMS chip.

3

Hold the Handles

Grip the two thread handles firmly, one in each hand, getting ready to spin the device.

4

Wind the Threads

Rotate the handles to wind the threads, building up potential energy for a powerful, high-speed spin.

5

Initiate Spinning

Pull your hands apart horizontally to release the tension, causing the chip to spin rapidly. Continue this motion to maintain rotation.

6

Maintain Rotation

Repeat the pull-and-release motion to keep the centrifuge spinning at the required speed until the blood components are separated.
